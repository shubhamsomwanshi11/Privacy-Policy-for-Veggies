Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up or sign in, we collect basic profile information such as your name and email address. If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google account information as permitted by Google’s authentication service.

App Content You Provide

The App allows you to create and manage goals, diary entries, categories, reminders and related content. This information is stored securely on your device and, when signed in, may be synced to cloud services to enable backup and multi-device access.

Usage and Device Data

We may collect limited usage information (such as general app interactions) and device details required to deliver features like notifications and background refresh. We do not sell personal data.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Sync and back up your goals and diary entries across devices.
  • Send optional push notifications and reminders when enabled.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience.
  • Communicate important updates about the App.

Data Storage and Security

Aspire uses secure storage on your device and trusted cloud services for synchronization and backup. We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers solely to operate the App, for example:

  • Authentication and account management (e.g., Firebase Authentication)
  • Cloud data storage and sync (e.g., Firebase/Firestore)
  • Sign-in with Google (Google Sign-In)
  • Push notifications (e.g., Expo Notifications)

These providers process your data on our behalf and according to their own privacy terms. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can access and update your profile information in the App.
  • You can enable/disable notifications in your device settings and within the App.
  • You may delete local data by clearing the app’s storage on your device.
  • To request deletion of cloud-synced data, please contact us (see Contact Us).

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App’s features. You can request deletion of your data as described above.

Children’s Privacy

Aspire is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Effective date” above. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the revised policy.
